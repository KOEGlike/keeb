This guide will not cover how you should write your firmware in full detail. There are a bunch of other resources that cover this topic really well.

We recommend that you use **QMK**, since it has a lot of cool features. [Here](https://docs.qmk.fm/newbs) is a tutorial for getting started with QMK.

## The Basics

1. **Set up your environment** — install QMK and its dependencies
2. **Build your first firmware** — configure your keymap and compile
3. **Flash firmware** — upload the compiled firmware to your Pico
