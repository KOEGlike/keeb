Welcome to **Keeb** — a [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com) you-ship-we-ship project where you learn to build your own mechanical keyboard from scratch.

## What You'll Build

A fully functional 60% mechanical keyboard, including:

- **Custom PCB** — designed for this project
- **Mechanical switches** — your choice of linear, tactile, or clicky
- **Keycaps** — a clean, no-nonsense keycap set
- **Case & plate** — everything to house your board
- **USB-C cable** — to connect to your machine

## How It Works

1. **Read through these docs** — learn about the parts, the process, and what to expect
2. **Follow the build guide** — step-by-step instructions from unboxing to typing. Then Add your own twist if you want! The crazier, the better.
3. **We ship you the parts** — once you're ready, we send everything to your door
4. **Build it** — solder, assemble, flash, and you've got a custom keyboard

## Prerequisites

No prior experience required. If you can hold a soldering iron, you can do this. The guide covers everything from basic soldering technique to firmware flashing.
