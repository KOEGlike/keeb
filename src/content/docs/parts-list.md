Here's everything that comes in your Keeb kit. You don't need to source anything yourself — we ship it all.

| Part | Quantity | Notes |

## Tools You'll Need

These are **not** included — you'll need to have or borrow:

- **Soldering iron** — any temperature-controlled iron works (if your PCB isn't hot-swap)
- **Solder** — 60/40 or 63/37 leaded solder recommended for beginners
- **Switch puller** — helpful but not required
- **Keycap puller** — included with most keycap sets

> **Note:** The default PCB uses hot-swap sockets, so no soldering is required for switch installation. Soldering is only needed if you want to add LEDs or modify the PCB.

## Switch Options

When you sign up, you'll choose your switch type:

- **Linear** — smooth keystroke, no bump (e.g., Gateron Yellow)
- **Tactile** — a bump you can feel (e.g., Gateron Brown)
- **Clicky** — a bump you can feel AND hear (e.g., Gateron Blue)

Not sure? Tactile is a good starting point for most people.
